About Us

Vision

Our vision as DiamondCut Motors is to allow our customers to enjoy luxury cars, supercars, and high-end cars both as car enthusiasts and as actual, willing customers. To this end, we at DiamondCut Motors try to resell every car at a reasonable and affordable price, relevant to today's market, to satisfy our vision that everyone should try a luxury car; to experience something that only appears once in a lifetime; and perhaps, acquire that experience forevermore. We believe that cars from prestigious brands shouldn't just be limited to the rich. Everyone should enjoy life. Nothing is impossible; even owning a luxury car.

Mission

Our mission as DiamondCut Motors is to import as many luxury cars with customer comfortability in mind, and resell them to out to customers at a reasonable and fair price, worldwide. By doing this, we can establish a connection between DiamondCut Motors and new customers, car enthusiasts, and many more potential customers all across the world, by becoming a baseline for what a dealership should stand for. Not only that, we aim to make it our mission to have our customers enjoy the luxury car they admire, both as car enthusiasts and as a proud car owner.

History of DiamondCut Motors

DiamondCut Motors was first established in 1988, starting as a small repair shop fixing motorbikes. Despite what seemed like a crazy idea at the time, DiamondCut Motors took a gamble, as the company purchasing our first luxury car; albeit broken and in disrepair; both as a centerpiece of the then small shop as well as to promote our repair capabilities by fixing the luxury car. Our repair job left our customers stunned, and many potential buyers came looking for us in order to score a luxury car of their own. Though the money from selling this repaired luxury car didn't compare to the usual standard, this event set ourselves off on a journey that would transform DiamondCut Motors into what it is today: a luxury car dealership, handling both reselling and repair of old luxury cars, in order to share the joy of luxury cars with other car enthusiasts and car owners alike across the world. Hence, DiamondCut Motors was reborn then and there. As time went on, we expanded from Indonesia to the UK, and later America, UAE, and Japan. As of right now, we plan on further expansions to accomodate more and more of our customers from all across the world.
